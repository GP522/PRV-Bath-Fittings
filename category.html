A statement in precision and purity, the PRV RAINÉ Overhead Shower transforms water into a sensorial experience. Crafted from solid brass and finished with meticulous detailing, it delivers a balanced, full-bodied flow that feels natural yet indulgent. The integrated AirMix™ system enriches water with air, creating a soft, rain-like cascade while conserving pressure and flow.

Engineered for durability and effortless maintenance, its anti-limescale nozzles ensure lasting performance and pristine appearance. Combining innovation with timeless design, RAINÉ exemplifies PRV’s philosophy — where water meets design in perfect harmony.
