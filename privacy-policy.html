Privacy Policy

Last updated january 2, 2025

At PRV Bath Fittings, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website, interact with our brand, or contact us through any of our digital channels.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information – such as your name, email address, phone number, and company details when you fill out enquiry forms, request a catalogue, or contact us.
  • Usage Data – including your browser type, IP address, pages visited, and time spent on our site, collected through analytics tools and cookies.
  • Communication Data – messages, feedback, or correspondence you send to us via contact forms or email.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and process catalogue or product requests.
  • Improve our website, design experience, and service offerings.
  • Send relevant updates, brand news, or invitations (only if you’ve opted in).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and collect aggregated data on website usage. You can disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the site may not function properly if you do.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your personal information is stored securely and only accessible by authorized personnel. We implement appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites for your convenience. PRV Bath Fittings is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external websites.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at info@prv.com. You can also unsubscribe from our communications using the link provided in any promotional email.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The revised policy will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
