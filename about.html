PRV Story

The very reason for our existence is experiment with innovation. It takes an instinct for thinking creatively and for initiating changes and challenging new goals.

We combine style and grace.

A sophisticated designing house and state-of-the-art manufacturing process gives the company a enviable edge. Constant innovation and investment in quality are two distinguishing features that has propelled our company to such heights.

We pride ourselves on being a customer oriented company with an emphasis on quality control and innovation. We have come a long way up in a short span of time only because of the patronage of our customers. Our strength is in the satisfaction of our valuable customers.

Our Vision

Design brings emotion, balance, and expression. Engineering brings precision, performance, and consistency.

Together, they create the language of water.
Our vision is to shape how people experience water through products that blend technology, craftsmanship, and timeless design. By refining every curve, finish, and flow, we turn simple moments into rituals of calm and beauty.

We’re building more than fittings. Alongside architects, designers, and dreamers, we’re building a new standard for living: where innovation complements artistry, where spaces feel personal and purposeful, and where every drop reflects care, precision, and elegance.

About PRV Group

PRV Group stands as a symbol of innovation, craftsmanship, and trust in the bath and sanitary industry. From precision-engineered faucets to advanced plumbing solutions, every brand under the PRV name reflects our commitment to design excellence and uncompromising quality.

Driven by a legacy of manufacturing expertise and a vision for modern living, PRV Group continues to expand its footprint across India and beyond. Creating products that elevate everyday experiences and redefine the way water interacts with design.
