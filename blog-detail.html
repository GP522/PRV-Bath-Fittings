Behind the Finish: Crafting
Surfaces That Endure

BY MAX JUNESTRAND — MAY 21, 2025

PRV, the design-led bath fittings company redefining modern luxury through craftsmanship and innovation, has announced a major expansion of its manufacturing and design capabilities — strengthening its position as one of India’s fastest-growing premium bath brands.

The expansion follows a period of exceptional growth, driven by the success of PRV’s latest faucet and shower collections, and increasing adoption among architects, interior designers, and hospitality developers globally. The move marks a strategic step toward scaling innovation, product depth, and design collaboration under one integrated ecosystem.

With the introduction of its new Experience & Innovation Center and a state-of-the-art production facility in Gujarat, PRV now combines design, prototyping, and advanced finishing technologies within a single vertical. This investment reinforces the company’s commitment to precision engineering, sustainable materials, and timeless aesthetics — cornerstones of the PRV philosophy.

PRV products are already featured in landmark residential and commercial projects across India, Dubai, and Singapore. With a growing presence in over 25 cities and partnerships with leading architects and developers, PRV continues to set new benchmarks in the bath fittings industry.

Operating primarily out of Mumbai and Delhi with satellite design studios in Pune and Bengaluru, PRV’s team of over 200 specialists — spanning product design, R&D, engineering, and quality control — work together to craft fittings that balance performance, purity, and artistry.
